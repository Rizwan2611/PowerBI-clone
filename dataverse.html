Power BI and Dataverse

Integrate Power BI with Microsoft Dataverse to build powerful business applications and reports with a unified data platform.

What is Dataverse?

Dataverse is a cloud-based data storage and management service that allows you to securely store and manage data used by business applications.

  • **Unified Data Storage:** A single, secure place for all your business data.
  • **Robust Security:** Role-based security and fine-grained access control.
  • **Scalability:** Scales to meet the needs of your business.
  • **Integration:** Easily integrates with Power Apps, Power Automate, and Power BI.

Benefits with Power BI

Connecting Power BI to Dataverse provides several advantages:

  • **Real-time Insights:** Access to live data for up-to-the-minute reporting.
  • **Simplified Data Access:** Easy data connection without complex ETL processes.
  • **Enhanced Security:** Leverage Dataverse's security model directly in Power BI.
  • **Seamless Experience:** Build and analyze data within the Microsoft ecosystem.
