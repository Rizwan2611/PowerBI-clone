Power BI Connectors

Connect Power BI to hundreds of data sources, on-premises and in the cloud, to get a comprehensive view of your business.

Key Connectors

Power BI offers a vast library of pre-built connectors for popular services and databases:

  • **Cloud Services:** Azure, Salesforce, Dynamics 365, Google Analytics, Facebook, Mailchimp.
  • **Databases:** SQL Server, Oracle, MySQL, PostgreSQL, Amazon Redshift.
  • **Files:** Excel, CSV, JSON, XML, PDF.
  • **Other Sources:** Web (HTML, JSON), OData feeds, SharePoint lists.

Custom Connectors

If a direct connector isn't available, you can build custom connectors to integrate with any data source that has an API.

  • **Power Query SDK:** Develop your own connectors using a flexible SDK.
  • **REST APIs:** Connect to web services with RESTful interfaces.
